Article of the Week

Travelling - By Shajida Akthar on 10th March 16

This week's article is about travelling. I can't say I've been to many countries but I intend on doing so once I feel stable in my career and have a travel partner. I want to let the pictures do the talking of all the places I'd like to visit and why.

I'll also be covering some of the activities I want to tick off my bucket list at some point in my life. Right now if I could, I would pack my bags and go for it - Around the world in 80 days?

My Places to Visit

1. Maldives

I've met a lot of people that have said they've wanted to visit this place. I mean look at the stunning view, it looks like paradise. This would be a place I'd chill out and relax for a few days doing nothing at all.

2. Hong Kong

Hong Kong is apparently known as a shopper's paradise which is one of the reasons I'd love to visit. But another is the festivals, culture and colourful nightlife. Imagine staying at a penthouse with that view -->

3. Iceland

This is my number one place to visit and one thing I really want to see. The northern lights (Aurora Borealis). There's tonnes of beaches and hot places I want to be right now but a place like this trumps all I think.

4. Singapore

I remember the first picture of a rooftop pool someone showed me and it was in Singapore. Curiousity got the better of me and a quick google search got me very intrigued. A sky-high pool sounds heavenly.

5. Dubai

I can't say Im very adventurous but sky-diving over the palm Jumeirah in Dubai is an obstacle I'd like to face at one point. I've come across many clips of other people doing so but I want to try for the thrill and adrenaline rush.

Hope you enjoyed reading about some of my handpicked places to visit. You can place comments in the comments section or even post suggestions there for next week's article.
