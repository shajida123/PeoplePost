The Shard 11th June 2015

This was posted on the 3rd of March 2016 by Shajida Akthar

I'm a sucker for beautiful sunsets and sunrises and witnessed one of the best from London's highest. Bear in mind, this was around summer so sunsets peaked at around 9pm. My mates and I booked a trip to the top of the Shard for 8:30pm and arrived with enough time to watch the sunset at its best.

I obviously took tonnes of photos - As you would! Hope you enjoy :)
